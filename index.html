About Me

I'm Alicia Wu, a sound designer studying Electronic Production and Design with a focus on Sound Design for Video Games at Berklee College of Music. I discovered my passion for game audio during my first game jam in high school, where I became fascinated by how sound can shape emotion, storytelling, and player experience. Since then, I've been dedicated to creating immersive soundscapes that bring virtual worlds to life.

I work with industry standard tools such as Wwise, FMOD and Reaper to build interactive audio experiences for games and VR environments. Beyond sound design, I'm deeply interested in interdisciplinary art, which blends my favorite things (visuals, interactive media, immersive technologies). I also explore modular synthesis as part of my creative practice and outside the studio you'll often find me snowboarding.

Skills & Education

  • Specialization: Sound Design for Video Games
