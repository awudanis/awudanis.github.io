Extensions of Soil

The gallery is enveloped in a woven meshwork of Tyvek, a spun fibrous material most commonly used as a protective skin for buildings in process, that evolves over the course of the installation through participatory weaving. Sculptures for Soil Drawings animate the surfaces of the installation with shadow projections of live drawing. Trio for Strings acts as the spore, first heard in its premiere performance on Nov 7, and becomes a source for iteration through improvisation in response to live weaving and soil drawing in the gallery.
